As a little child, I was always fascinated by how things worked, from the simplest toys to the most complex machines. As a child, i loved to take things apart and put them back together again, trying to figure out how they worked. When it came time to choose a career, i knew that i wanted to be an engineer. I was drawn to electrical engineering because it was a challenging and rewarding field that allowed me to use my creativity and problem-solving skills to design and build electrical systems. I enjoyed the challenge of the coursework and the satisfaction of completing complex projects. However, as i got closer to completition of my high school, i started to realize that electrical engineering was not the right field for me. I found myself more interested in the software side of engineering. I loved the creativity and flexibility of software development, and i was excited about the potential to use my skills to create innovative new products and services. I decided to make a switch to software engineering. And now am pursuing my bachelors Degree in Software Engineering at Bugema University.
